Privacy Policy for Qwik Alarm

Last updated: March 19, 2024

Qwik is a simple, privacy-focused alarm app that helps you quickly set alarms without any unnecessary complications.

Information We Don't Collect:

  • We don't collect any personal information
  • We don't track your location
  • We don't access your contacts
  • We don't share any data with third parties
  • We don't use analytics or tracking tools
  • We don't serve advertisements

Information We Store Locally:

  • Alarm preferences (minutes/hours setting)
  • Active alarm information (temporarily, until the alarm is dismissed)

App Permissions:

  • Alarm permission: Required only to set and manage alarms using your device's default clock app
  • Storage permission: Used only to save your preferences locally on your device

Data Storage:

All data is stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences system. We do not transmit any data to external servers or cloud services.

Third-Party Services:

Qwik uses your device's default alarm clock app to set alarms. We don't have access to or control over how your device's alarm app handles data. Please refer to your device manufacturer's privacy policy for information about the default alarm app.

Children's Privacy:

Qwik is suitable for users of all ages and does not collect any personal information from children or adults.

Changes to This Policy:

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Open Source:

Qwik is open source software. You can review our code on GitHub.

Contact:

For questions about this privacy policy or the Qwik app, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.

This privacy policy is effective as of March 19, 2024
